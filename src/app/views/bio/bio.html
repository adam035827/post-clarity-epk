About Post Clarity

Post Clarity is Kansas City's premier cover band, delivering high-energy performances that bring your favorite hits to life. With a diverse setlist spanning multiple decades and genres, we specialize in creating unforgettable experiences for corporate events, weddings, festivals, and private parties.

Our talented musicians combine technical excellence with genuine passion, ensuring every performance connects with audiences of all ages. From classic rock anthems to contemporary pop hits, Post Clarity adapts to any venue and vibe, making every event memorable.

Based in the heart of Kansas City, we've built a reputation for professionalism, versatility, and delivering performances that keep dance floors packed and guests talking long after the last note fades.

  • Professional sound and lighting equipment
  • Customizable setlists to match your event
  • Experienced performers with years of live performance expertise
  • Full liability insurance and professional contracts
